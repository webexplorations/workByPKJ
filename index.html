Peter Kendrick Johnson

Peter Kendrick Johnson lives in Southern Minnesota and has been drawing and painting for several years. Every Saturday he co-hosts the Urban Sketching group at Sidetracked, a poetry and art supply shop in downtown Mankato, where his watercolor and mixed-media notecards are also available.
