About the Artist

Peter Kendrick Johnson lives in Southern Minnesota and has been drawing and painting for several years. Every Saturday he is co-host with the Urban Sketching group held at Sidetracked, a poetry and art supply shop in downtown Mankato.

In his past lives he has been a photographer in the Navy, a programmer, president of a corporation, and a computer programming instructor.
